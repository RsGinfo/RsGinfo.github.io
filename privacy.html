Note on Parental Access to Data

We do not collect personal information from users. We only gather anonymous technical data such as crash reports and feature usage counts that cannot be linked back to individuals. COPPA grants parents rights to access or delete personal information, but because none is collected, there's nothing applicable under those rights.
